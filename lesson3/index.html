Site Purpose

Weather Wonder will provide current and future weather conditions for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. This site will also provide weather realted news and events for local residents and visitors to use when planning their outdoor activities. The site will be optimized for multiple viewport sizes to allow users to access the inmformation on most devices.
