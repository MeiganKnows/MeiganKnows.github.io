Target Audience

Weather Wonder is intend primarily for those people who live in the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven communitites, or for those people who are planning a visit to the area and want or need to know the current or future weather conditions. Other users would included people who have an interest in weather realated news and events of the area, or just weather information and news in general. These people bridge most socio-econimic classes, and are not limited to any particular occupation or demographic, as long as they have access to the website. Users will be able to access the site from any internet enabled device.
