Overview

Purpose

Exciting Expeditions organizes and facilitates rafting trips for private parties and corporate team building. Their website allows potential and actual customers to view excursion dates and locations, and obtain necessary information to plan their own Exciting Expedition.

Audience

Intended for use by people who are looking to book a rafting trip. Includes both corprate and private clients.

Typography

Heading Font: Abril Fatface

The bold, formal look of this serif font, contrasts nicely with the light feel of Gayathri without being too formal.

Paragraph Font: Gayathri

Gives the air of an outdoor camp or cabin feel whithout being too wimsical or unreadable.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, Exciting Expedidtions, offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventures you are seeking, Exciting Expeditions can make it happen for you.
