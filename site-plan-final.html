Purpose

Have you ever refinanced your mortgage but not wanted to travel to the title company to sign your paperwork? Good news, now a notary public trained in completing lender and title document signings can come to a convenient location, such as your home, at a time that works for your schedule. Maybe you donâ€™t need real estate documents signed, but are selling a car? Or finalizing your will or other estate documents? We can also help with these documents your location and convenience. There are lots more services that we offer. Check out the website to find what services we offer title companies and individuals. There will also be information about how you could also become a notary public signing agent.
We provide on-demand notary services at remote loactions for clients who need documents notarized. This includes all title documents, such as mortgages, deeds and other real estate documents. We also provide mobile signing services for all other documents that require notarization, including wills, trusts, car titles, financial documents, permission slips, etc. The website provides users with the rates and services provided, including the geographic areas that we service. There will be a contact page for clients to request an appointment or get a service quote. Additional information will be provided for people who would like to start their own mobile signing buisness.

Audience

The intended audience for this site is those who are seeking notarial services in the East Valley area of The Phoenix Metro area. While people of any socio-economic class might have need of a notary, those who typically seek out a mobile notary are those who own property and would prefer not to take time off from work to travel to the title office to complete their paperwork. These people are also those who need to make their appointment outside of typical business hours. Other people who frequently seek out a moblie notary are those who have personal documents to sign that local banks are unable to accomadate, such as wills or trust. Antipated users also include those who are homebound or in assisted living facilities, and even those who find it hard to travel to a location, such as a bank, to see a notary.
