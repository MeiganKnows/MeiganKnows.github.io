Milage fees equal to the the amount allowed by Arizona State public employees may be charged. Arizona law sets the fees that notaries may charge for their service. Arizona Revised Statute §§ 41-316 states, "A notary can charge no more than $10 per signature. Notary fees are set in rule (A.A.C. R2-12-1102)." Service or transaction fees shall not be charged. .
