Meigan, The Notary Fee Schedule

General Notary Work

$10 per notary signature for acknowledgemnts, jurats, copy certifications, and oaths/affirmations.

Arizona law sets the fees that notaries may charge for their service. Arizona Revised Statute §§ 41-316 states, "A notary can charge no more than $10 per signature. Notary fees are set in rule (A.A.C. R2-12-1102)." Service or transaction fees shall not be charged. Milage fees equal to the the amount allowed by Arizona State public employees may be charged.
